Success Stories

CUSTOMER SUCCESS

Transit Hub Convenience Kiosk Deployment

City of Greenfield Public Transit Department - The City of Greenfield was renovating its main downtown bus terminal, which served 12,000 daily commuters but had no on-site retail options, forcing travelers to walk several blocks to purchase snacks or essentials before their trip. WAYMOTIVE INC designed, installed, and operated two custom transit kiosks in the terminal, aligned with the city’s goal of improving the commuter experience and supporting local businesses. We worked with the transit department to adjust operating hours to match the terminal’s 5 a.m. to 1 a.m. schedule, and curated inventory to include local snack brands from Greenfield-area small businesses alongside standard convenience items.

24/7 Campus Convenience Outlet

Northwood University Student Government Association - Northwood University, a mid-sized public university with 15,000 undergraduate students, had limited on-campus retail options that closed by 10 p.m., leaving students with no place to purchase study supplies, snacks, or essentials during late-night study sessions, particularly during exam periods. WAYMOTIVE INC partnered with the university and student government to open a 4,000 square foot campus convenience outlet near the main dormitory complex, with operating hours extended to 2 a.m. on weekdays and 24/7 operation during midterm and final exam periods. We incorporated student feedback into the inventory, adding affordable meal options, study supplies, and international snack brands requested by the student body, and accepted the university’s campus meal plan as a payment method.

Highway Rest Stop Convenience Store Upgrade

Interstate Highway Authority Region 3 - The Interstate Highway Authority’s Region 3 operated 8 rest stops along a 300-mile stretch of interstate highway, with outdated, understocked convenience stores that had limited operating hours and received consistent complaints from travelers, particularly long-haul truck drivers. WAYMOTIVE INC won the contract to upgrade and operate all 8 rest stop convenience locations, renovating each store to add expanded food options, truck driver supplies, and 24/7 year-round operation. We also added free public Wi-Fi, comfortable seating areas, and upgraded restroom facilities as part of the project, aligned with the highway authority’s goal of improving traveler safety and reducing fatigued driving.
