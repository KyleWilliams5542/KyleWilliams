Fresh Prepared Meals & Sandwiches

Ready-to-eat fresh sandwiches, salads, wraps, and hot meal options made daily with local ingredients for quick, satisfying meals on the go.

Key Features

  • ✓ Daily preparation using local, seasonal ingredients
  • ✓ Rotating daily specials and extensive dietary-inclusive options
  • ✓ Three daily restocks during peak meal times for maximum freshness
  • ✓ 100% of unsold prepared food donated to local food banks
  • ✓ In-store microwave access for fast heating of hot meal options

Product Details

Our Fresh Prepared Meals & Sandwiches selection is prepared daily on-site by our trained food service staff, using ingredients sourced from local farms and bakeries whenever possible. The assortment includes vegetarian, vegan, and meat-based options, with rotating daily specials that feature seasonal ingredients. All prepared food items are clearly labeled with ingredient lists, allergen information, and calorie counts to help customers make informed choices. We restock prepared meals three times a day during peak meal times (morning, lunch, and evening) to ensure freshness, and all unsold prepared food is donated to local food banks at the end of each day rather than being thrown away. For customers looking for a hot meal, many locations also offer a selection of ready-to-heat options including soups, pasta dishes, and breakfast burritos that can be heated in-store in under two minutes.
