University Campus Convenience Outlet

Student-focused convenience locations on college and university campuses, offering extended hours and student-friendly pricing for busy students and faculty.

  • Located near dorms, lecture halls, and student centers
  • Student-exclusive discounts and flexible payment options including campus meal plans
  • Late-night operating hours aligned with student study schedules
  • Selection of study supplies, energy drinks, and affordable meal options
  • Bulk snack packs for group study sessions

Product Details

Our university campus convenience outlets are tailored specifically for the needs of college students, faculty, and staff, with locations placed in high-traffic areas of campus including near dormitories, lecture halls, and student centers. We recognize that student schedules are highly irregular, with late-night study sessions, early morning classes, and weekend activities that don’t align with standard retail hours, so all our campus locations are open until at least 2 a.m. on weekdays, and 24 hours a day during exam periods to support students during busy academic periods. Inventory for campus locations includes all standard everyday essentials, plus a wide range of study supplies (notebooks, pens, calculators, etc.), energy drinks and coffee, affordable pre-packaged meals and snacks, and bulk snack packs ideal for group study sessions or dorm events. We accept all major payment methods, including university campus meal plans, and offer exclusive 10% discounts for students with a valid student ID, making our products accessible for students on tight budgets. We also regularly update our inventory based on feedback from student government groups, adding items that students request, from international snacks to specific school supplies. In addition to retail, our campus locations frequently partner with student organizations to host study breaks during exam periods, offering free coffee and snacks to students, and sponsor campus events throughout the academic year. We hire students for part-time positions at all our campus locations, providing flexible work schedules that accommodate class schedules and exam periods. These locations also serve as pickup points for our student pre-order service, allowing students to order items through our app between classes and pick them up on their way to their next lecture or dorm.
