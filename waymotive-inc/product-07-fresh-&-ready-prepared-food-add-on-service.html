Fresh & Ready Prepared Food Add-On Service

An optional add-on service for all our convenience locations, offering fresh, locally sourced prepared meals, baked goods, and hot beverage options.

  • Daily fresh preparation with local, high-quality ingredients
  • Dietary inclusive options including vegan, gluten-free, and low-sugar meals
  • Customizable hot coffee and specialty drink bar
  • Affordable pricing comparable to standard grocery store prepared foods
  • Zero-waste packaging options available

Product Details

Our Fresh & Ready prepared food add-on service is available in 70% of our convenience locations, providing customers with access to fresh, high-quality prepared meals without the need to visit a full grocery store or restaurant. All meals and baked goods are prepared daily, either on-site in larger locations or delivered each morning from local certified commercial kitchens, using as many locally sourced ingredients as possible from regional farms and food producers. We rotate our menu every 4 weeks, offering different meal options throughout the year to align with seasonal produce and customer preferences. The menu includes a wide range of options to accommodate different dietary needs, including clearly labeled vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, low-sugar, and low-sodium meals, so customers with dietary restrictions can easily find options that work for them. In addition to prepared meals, the service includes a full hot beverage bar with freshly brewed coffee, espresso drinks, tea, and hot chocolate, all customizable with a range of dairy and non-dairy milk options and flavorings. We also offer fresh baked goods including muffins, pastries, and breads, delivered daily from local bakeries. All Fresh & Ready items are priced competitively, with most meals costing less than comparable options from fast casual restaurants, making them an affordable option for customers looking for a quick, fresh meal. We offer zero-waste packaging options for customers who bring their own reusable containers, and all single-use packaging used for the service is either compostable or recyclable, aligning with our commitment to reducing environmental impact. We regularly collect feedback on the menu from customers, adding new items based on popular demand and removing items that are less popular to keep the selection relevant for each local market.
