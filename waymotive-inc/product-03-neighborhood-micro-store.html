Neighborhood Micro-Store

Small-format convenience locations in dense urban residential areas, offering a targeted selection of the most frequently purchased everyday items for local residents.

  • Located within 5-minute walking distance of most surrounding homes
  • Hyper-local inventory tailored to specific neighborhood preferences
  • Same-day in-store pickup for online pre-orders
  • Pet supplies and local specialty items not available in standard convenience stores
  • Community-focused events and local product pop-ups

Product Details

Our neighborhood micro-stores are designed for dense urban residential areas where full-sized grocery stores may be scarce or a long walk away. Typically under 1,000 square feet, these small locations are placed to be within a 5-minute walk for most households in the surrounding area, making them the ideal option for residents who need to pick up just one or two items without a long trip. We work closely with local community groups when selecting inventory for each micro-store, ensuring that we carry the specific brands, dietary options, and household items preferred by the residents in that specific neighborhood, including hard-to-find cultural and specialty products. In addition to our standard range of snacks, beverages, and household essentials, our micro-stores carry a selection of pet supplies, basic hardware items, and local artisanal products sourced from small businesses in the surrounding area. We also offer a free pre-order service, where customers can place an order for items through our mobile app and pick it up in-store in as little as 15 minutes, eliminating the need to wait for restocks or search for items on shelves. This service is particularly popular for customers with mobility limitations or those who are shopping for specific items during busy periods. Beyond retail, our micro-stores serve as small community hubs, hosting occasional pop-up events for local food producers, collecting donations for local food banks, and offering free community resources such as public water bottle filling stations and phone charging ports. We prioritize hiring staff who live in the local neighborhood, so our team knows regular customers by name and can provide personalized recommendations and service. All micro-stores are open 18 hours a day, 7 days a week, to accommodate the varied schedules of urban residents.
