Product Details

Our Personal Care & Health Essentials assortment includes common over-the-counter medications such as pain relievers, cold and flu remedies, allergy medication, antacids, and first aid supplies, all sold in appropriate small-pack sizes for immediate use. We also stock oral care products, deodorant, shampoo and conditioner, feminine hygiene products, and basic skin care items to cover unexpected needs when customers run out of products or are away from home. All medications are stored in temperature-controlled cabinets and checked regularly for expiration dates to ensure safety and efficacy. We offer a mix of name-brand and affordable generic options for most medications and personal care products, making essential health items accessible to customers on all budgets. Our staff are trained to answer basic questions about product use, and we post clear information about nearby pharmacies for customers who require prescription medications or more specialized health advice.
