Contactless Self-Service Convenience Module

A fully automated, 24/7 self-service convenience solution for locations where a full staffed store is not feasible, including small apartment buildings, industrial sites, and remote facilities.

  • Fully automated operation 24/7 with no on-site staff required
  • Contactless payment and app-based access for maximum security
  • Inventory of 200+ most popular convenience items
  • Remote inventory monitoring and daily restocking for high-traffic locations
  • Compact footprint that fits in spaces as small as 100 square feet

Product Details

Our contactless self-service convenience modules are an innovative solution for locations that cannot support a full staffed convenience store but still need access to everyday essentials, including small apartment buildings, large industrial work sites, remote office locations, and recreational facilities. These fully automated units require no on-site staff, operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and can be installed in spaces as small as 100 square feet, making them extremely versatile for hard-to-serve locations. Customers access the unit either through a contactless credit card tap or our mobile app, with security cameras and access tracking to prevent theft and ensure safety. Each module is stocked with a curated selection of over 200 of our most popular items, including snacks, beverages, personal care items, over-the-counter medications, and basic household goods, with inventory tailored to the specific needs of the location. For example, modules in apartment buildings include more household items and late-night snacks, while modules in industrial work sites include more energy drinks, work gloves, and other supplies for laborers. Our remote monitoring system tracks inventory levels in real time, so our restocking team knows exactly when items need to be replenished, with high-traffic modules restocked daily and lower-traffic locations restocked 2–3 times a week. The self-service modules are designed for maximum ease of use: once inside, customers simply pick the items they want, scan them at the self-checkout terminal, pay with a contactless payment method, and exit. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes, and customers can report any issues with the unit or inventory directly through our mobile app, with our support team responding within 1 hour during regular business hours. This solution has been particularly successful in rural areas and small communities where full-sized retail locations are not economically viable, bringing convenient access to essential items to areas that previously had limited options.
