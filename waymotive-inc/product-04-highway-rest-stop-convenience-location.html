Highway Rest Stop Convenience Location

Full-service convenience stores located along major highway rest stops, serving long-haul drivers and road trippers with a wide range of travel and daily essentials.

  • Ample parking for passenger vehicles and commercial trucks
  • Extended hot food and beverage menu for long trips
  • Rest area amenities including clean public restrooms and seating areas
  • Truck driver supplies including travel gear and food options for long routes
  • Open 24/7, 365 days a year including all holidays

Product Details

Our highway rest stop convenience locations are specifically designed to serve people traveling long distances by road, including family road trippers and professional long-haul truck drivers. Located at major interstate rest stops across our operating region, these stores offer far more than standard convenience items, with amenities tailored to the unique needs of travelers who may be spending multiple hours or days on the road. All locations have dedicated parking spaces for both passenger vehicles and large commercial trucks, so drivers can stop quickly without struggling to find parking. Inventory for these locations includes all our standard convenience items, plus an expanded selection of hot meals, fresh coffee and energy drinks, travel-sized hygiene products, road trip snacks, emergency car supplies, and specialized products for commercial truck drivers including log books, seat cushions, and other long-haul essentials. We also offer free Wi-Fi and comfortable indoor seating areas, so travelers can take a break from driving, stretch their legs, and enjoy a meal before getting back on the road. All locations have clean, well-maintained public restrooms that are accessible 24 hours a day, even for customers who are not making a purchase. Every highway rest stop location is open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, including all major holidays, so we are always available for travelers regardless of their schedule. During periods of severe weather, these locations often serve as emergency resource stops, providing free water, warm drinks, and information for travelers who are stranded or delayed. We restock these locations multiple times a day, even during holidays and severe weather events, to ensure that we always have sufficient inventory for the high volume of travelers on major highways.
