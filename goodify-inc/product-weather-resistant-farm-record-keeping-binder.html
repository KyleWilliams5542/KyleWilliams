Weather-Resistant Farm Record Keeping Binder - Detailed Description

A durable, waterproof record keeping system for small-scale farmers to track planting dates, harvest yields, expenses, and sales, supporting informed business decisions and simplified tax reporting.

## Product Features

• Waterproof and tear-resistant cover and pages for use in farm field conditions
• Pre-formatted sections for crop logs, expense tracking, sales records, and pest management notes
• Includes 12 dividers for annual organization and 50 pre-printed log sheets
• Compatible with our free digital farm record keeping template for seamless data transfer
• Meets USDA record keeping requirements for organic certification audits
