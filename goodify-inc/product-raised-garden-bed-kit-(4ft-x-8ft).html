Raised Garden Bed Kit (4ft x 8ft) - Detailed Description

A tool-free assembly raised garden bed made from sustainably sourced cedar, designed to create an accessible, high-yield growing space for home gardeners with limited ground space or poor soil quality.

## Product Features

• Constructed from 100% FSC-certified western red cedar, naturally rot-resistant without chemical treatments
• Assembles in under 30 minutes with no additional tools required
• 12-inch height provides ample root depth for vegetables and perennial plants
• Includes a fitted weed barrier fabric to reduce maintenance
• Backed by a 5-year structural warranty
