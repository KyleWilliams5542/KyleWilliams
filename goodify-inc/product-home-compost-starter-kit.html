Home Compost Starter Kit - Detailed Description

A complete, odor-free composting system for small urban and suburban homes, designed to turn kitchen scraps and yard waste into nutrient-rich soil amendment for home gardens in as little as 8 weeks.

## Product Features

• 5-gallon BPA-free compost bin with dual-chamber design for continuous composting
• Includes organic compost activator and a detailed guide for beginner composters
• Sealed lid and ventilation system eliminate odors and pest attraction
• Suitable for both indoor and outdoor use in apartments, townhomes, and single-family homes
• Diverts up to 200 pounds of household waste from landfills annually
