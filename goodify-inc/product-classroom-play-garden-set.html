Classroom Play Garden Set - Detailed Description

An indoor/outdoor pretend gardening set that teaches young children about plant life cycles, food origins, and environmental care through interactive, imaginative play. Suitable for both classroom and outdoor play areas.

## Product Features

• UV-resistant and weatherproof for year-round outdoor use
• Includes realistic toy vegetables, child-sized tools, and a reusable growth tracking chart
• Supports social-emotional learning through collaborative play activities
• Easy to sanitize between uses for shared classroom environments
• Recommended for children ages 3 to 6
