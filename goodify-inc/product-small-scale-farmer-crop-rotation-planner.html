Small-Scale Farmer Crop Rotation Planner - Detailed Description

A comprehensive, printable and digital planning tool designed for 1-10 acre farms, helping growers improve soil health, reduce pest pressure, and maximize annual yield through science-backed crop rotation strategies.

## Product Features

• Customizable for 1 to 10 acres of growing space with multiple field layout templates
• Includes 5 years of crop rotation schedules tailored to common vegetable and row crop operations
• Integrated soil nutrient tracking and pest management recommendation sections
• Compatible with both organic and conventional farming practices
• Comes with free annual updates for new crop varieties and best practices
