Portable Small Farm Irrigation System - Detailed Description

A lightweight, easy-to-install drip irrigation system designed for 1-5 acre small farms, reducing water usage by up to 60% compared to traditional sprinkler irrigation while improving crop uniformity.

## Product Features

• Modular design allows for customization to fit any field shape or crop layout
• Low-flow emitters deliver consistent water directly to plant root zones
• Compatible with standard garden hoses and small agricultural water pumps
• UV-resistant components have a 7+ year outdoor lifespan
• Reduces weed growth by limiting water exposure to targeted planting areas

Suitable for various industries and usage scenarios, meeting the needs of different customers.
