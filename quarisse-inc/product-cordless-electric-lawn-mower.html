Cordless Electric Lawn Mower - Detailed Description

An eco-friendly 20-inch cordless electric lawn mower powered by a 40V lithium-ion battery, ideal for noise-sensitive neighborhoods and small to medium-sized yards.

## Product Features

• Brushless motor with 60 minutes of continuous run time per full charge
• 7-position height adjustment and mulching, bagging, and side discharge options
• Quiet operation at only 75 decibels, 50% quieter than comparable gas models
• Weather-resistant construction for use in light rain conditions

Suitable for various industries and usage scenarios, meeting the needs of different customers.
