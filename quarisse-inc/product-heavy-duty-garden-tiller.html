Heavy-Duty Garden Tiller - Detailed Description

A 16-inch rear-tine garden tiller powered by a 196cc 4-cycle engine, designed for breaking new ground, preparing garden beds, and mixing soil and compost.

## Product Features

• 4 forward and 1 reverse speed settings for optimal control over different soil types
• Adjustable tilling depth up to 8 inches for custom soil preparation
• Counter-rotating tines that easily break through compacted soil and clay
• Pneumatic 10-inch wheels for improved traction in uneven garden terrain
