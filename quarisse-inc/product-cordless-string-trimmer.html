Cordless String Trimmer - Detailed Description

A 13-inch cordless string trimmer and edger combo, perfect for trimming grass around fences, walkways, and flower beds, with a rotating head that converts to an edger in seconds.

## Product Features

• 40V lithium-ion battery provides 45 minutes of continuous runtime per charge
• Automatic feed spool that advances line as needed without bumping
• Adjustable telescopic shaft to accommodate users of different heights
• Weighs only 6.8 pounds for reduced fatigue during prolonged use
