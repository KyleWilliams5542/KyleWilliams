14-Inch Cordless Chainsaw - Detailed Description

A compact 14-inch cordless chainsaw powered by a 40V battery, ideal for light cutting tasks, pruning, and small firewood processing, with no gas, fumes, or pull cords required.

## Product Features

• Tool-free chain tensioning system for quick adjustments in the field
• Brushless motor that delivers 30% more torque and 50% longer runtime than brushed motors
• Kickback brake and low-kickback chain for enhanced user safety
• Up to 60 cuts per charge on 4x4 pressure-treated lumber
