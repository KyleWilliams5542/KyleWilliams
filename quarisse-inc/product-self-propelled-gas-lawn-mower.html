Self-Propelled Gas Lawn Mower - Detailed Description

A 21-inch self-propelled gas lawn mower designed for residential and light commercial use, delivering consistent cutting performance across uneven terrain and thick grass.

• 160cc 4-cycle engine with low emissions and 50+ minute run time per tank
• 6 adjustable cutting heights ranging from 1.2 to 3.5 inches
• Rear-wheel drive system for improved traction on slopes and wet grass
• Foldable handle for compact storage in small garages or sheds
