7500W Portable Generator - Detailed Description

A heavy-duty 7500W portable generator designed for emergency home backup, job site power, and outdoor recreational use, providing reliable electricity during outages or off-grid activities.

• 420cc OHV engine with 12 hours of run time at 50% load on a 6.6 gallon fuel tank
• Multiple outlets including 4 120V AC, 1 240V AC, and 2 USB ports for versatile use
• Low-oil automatic shutoff and overload protection for enhanced safety
• Roll cage design and never-flat wheels for easy transport across rough terrain
