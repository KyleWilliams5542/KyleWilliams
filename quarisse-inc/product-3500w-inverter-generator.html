3500W Inverter Generator - Detailed Description

A lightweight 3500W inverter generator that produces clean, stable power for sensitive electronics, perfect for camping, tailgating, and powering small home appliances during outages.

## Product Features

• Ultra-quiet operation at 58 decibels at 25% load, suitable for use in campgrounds and residential areas
• Produces <3% THD (total harmonic distortion) to safely power laptops, phones, and medical devices
• 10 hours of run time at 25% load with fuel-efficient Eco Mode activated
• Weighs only 46 pounds with an integrated carry handle for easy transport
