About Us

QUARISSE INC is a leading provider of high-performance outdoor power equipment, serving both residential homeowners and professional landscapers across North America. We combine durable, rigorously tested products with expert after-sales support to help customers complete outdoor projects efficiently and safely.

Founded in 2012, QUARISSE INC started with a simple mission: to make high-quality outdoor power equipment accessible to all users, regardless of their experience level. We partner with trusted manufacturers and conduct in-house performance testing on every product we carry to ensure it meets our strict standards for durability, safety, and efficiency. Our team of industry experts provides personalized product recommendations, maintenance guidance, and responsive repair services to help customers get the most out of their investment. We are committed to sustainability, offering energy-efficient equipment options and operating a responsible recycling program for old tools and batteries. Today, we serve over 40,000 customers annually, with a 96% customer satisfaction rating that reflects our dedication to quality and support.

Student Success Stories

Fleet Equipment Upgrade for Commercial Landscaping Operations

Client: GreenScape Landscaping, Inc.

GreenScape Landscaping, a regional commercial landscaping company with 25 crew members, needed to replace their aging fleet of lawn mowers, trimmers, and blowers to improve efficiency and reduce maintenance costs. QUARISSE INC conducted a full assessment of their daily operations, recommended a mix of gas and cordless equipment to match their job requirements, and provided customized training for their entire team on proper use and maintenance.

  • Reduced weekly equipment maintenance time by 40% and cut fuel costs by 25% within the first 6 months
  • Improved crew productivity by 18%, allowing the company to take on 12 additional commercial contracts in their first year with the new equipment

Emergency Backup Power Solution for 120-Home Community Center

Client: Maple Ridge Residential Community

Maple Ridge, a residential community in Ohio, needed a reliable backup power solution for their 10,000 square foot community center, which serves as an emergency shelter during severe weather events. QUARISSE INC supplied and installed a 7500W portable generator paired with a transfer switch, and provided annual maintenance services to ensure the system was always ready for use.

  • Successfully powered the community center for 72 consecutive hours during a 2023 winter storm, providing heat, lighting, and charging facilities for over 80 residents
  • 100% uptime recorded in 3 years of annual performance testing, exceeding the community's reliability requirements

Equipment Package for Professional Tree Care Operations

Client: Timberline Tree Service

Timberline Tree Service, a small business specializing in tree removal and pruning, needed to expand their equipment inventory to take on larger residential and commercial tree care projects. QUARISSE INC provided a customized package including 20-inch gas chainsaws, hedge trimmers, and safety gear, along with ongoing maintenance support and part replacement services.

  • Reduced average project completion time by 30% due to the increased cutting power and reliability of the new equipment
  • Achieved 98% customer satisfaction rating on tree service projects in the first year, with zero equipment-related delays reported
