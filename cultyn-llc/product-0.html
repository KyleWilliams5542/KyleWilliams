Hand-Glazed Ceramic Table Vase Collection

Artisanal ceramic vases featuring unique hand-glazed finishes, perfect for displaying fresh bouquets or serving as standalone decorative accents on shelves, mantels, and dining tables.

Key Features

  • ✓ 100% handcrafted high-fired stoneware construction
  • ✓ Unique non-repeating hand-glazed finish on every piece
  • ✓ Waterproof, chip-resistant, and food-safe design
  • ✓ Available in 3 sizes and 4 neutral color options
  • ✓ Sustainable, lead-free, and non-toxic materials

Product Details

Our Hand-Glazed Ceramic Table Vase Collection is crafted by skilled artisans using high-fired stoneware clay, ensuring exceptional durability and resistance to chipping, cracking, and water damage. Each vase undergoes a multi-step glazing process that creates one-of-a-kind color variations and subtle texture differences, meaning no two pieces are exactly alike. Available in three versatile sizes (6-inch, 8-inch, and 10-inch) and a curated palette of matte terracotta, soft sage, muted blue, and crisp white, these vases complement nearly any interior design scheme from rustic farmhouse to contemporary minimalist.



Designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind, each vase features a wide, stable base to prevent tipping and a smooth, non-porous interior that is easy to clean and safe for fresh flowers. The neutral, earthy tones of the glazes enhance rather than overpower floral arrangements, while the organic shape of each vessel adds visual interest even when empty. Ideal for entryway consoles, bedroom dressers, office desks, or dining table centerpieces, these vases are versatile enough to adapt to seasonal decor changes and evolving style preferences. All pieces in this collection are food-safe, making them suitable for use as utensil holders in kitchens or serving vessels for dry snacks at gatherings.
