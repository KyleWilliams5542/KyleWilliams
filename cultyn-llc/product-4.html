Dimmable LED Table Lamp with Linen Shade

Sleek, modern table lamp featuring a solid wood base, dimmable LED bulb, and linen shade, providing warm, adjustable lighting for bedrooms, home offices, and living rooms.

Key Features

  • ✓ Solid FSC-certified oak or walnut wood base with natural oil finish
  • ✓ Built-in dimmable LED bulb (50,000 hour lifespan, 80% energy efficient)
  • ✓ Touch-sensitive 3-level brightness control (low, medium, high)
  • ✓ UL-certified electrical components for safety and reliability
  • ✓ Removable linen shade for easy cleaning and soft light diffusion

Product Details

Our Dimmable LED Table Lamp combines minimalist design with practical functionality, making it a versatile lighting solution for nearly any space. The base is crafted from solid FSC-certified oak or walnut wood, sanded to a smooth finish and treated with natural plant-based oil that highlights the natural grain of the wood. The tapered linen shade diffuses light evenly, eliminating harsh glares and creating a soft, warm ambient glow that is perfect for reading, working, or relaxing. The lamp includes a built-in dimmable LED bulb that lasts up to 50,000 hours, reducing energy consumption by 80% compared to traditional incandescent bulbs, and features a touch-sensitive control on the base that allows you to adjust brightness between three levels with a simple tap.



Designed with safety and convenience in mind, the lamp has a weighted base to prevent tipping, a 6-foot braided fabric cord that fits seamlessly with modern decor, and is compatible with most smart plugs for voice control integration. The compact 18-inch height makes it ideal for small bedside tables, narrow office desks, or entryway consoles, while the timeless design works with a wide range of interior styles from mid-century modern to Scandinavian and contemporary. The linen shade is removable for easy cleaning, and the solid wood base develops a rich patina over time, adding character to the piece with years of use. All electrical components are UL certified for safety and meet North American electrical standards.
