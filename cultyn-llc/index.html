About Us

Who We Are

Cultyn LLC is a premium home decor brand dedicated to crafting thoughtfully designed aesthetic products that transform ordinary living spaces into extraordinary personal sanctuaries. We blend timeless artistic elements with practical functionality to create pieces that reflect your unique style while seamlessly integrating with your daily lifestyle. Every item in our collection is curated to balance beauty, durability, and purpose, making elevated home design accessible to all who value intentional living.

Founded in 2019, Cultyn LLC was born from a simple belief: your home should be a reflection of who you are, not a generic template of current trends. Our team of in-house designers and craftspeople draws inspiration from global art movements, natural landscapes, and contemporary lifestyle needs to create pieces that feel both timeless and relevant. We prioritize ethical manufacturing practices, using sustainable materials whenever possible and partnering with small, family-owned production facilities that share our commitment to quality and fair labor. Unlike mass-market home decor brands that prioritize low costs over longevity, we design every item to last for years, both in terms of structural durability and aesthetic appeal. Whether you are furnishing a new apartment, refreshing a single room, or searching for the perfect gift, our collection offers pieces that align with diverse design preferences, from minimalist modern to warm bohemian and classic traditional. At Cultyn, we don’t just sell home decor — we help you create spaces where you feel truly at home.

Success Stories

Case Studies

Urban Rest Short-Term Rental Management

Premium Vacation Rental Property Furnishing

Urban Rest, a management company overseeing 42 luxury short-term rental properties across Austin, Texas, approached Cultyn LLC to source cohesive, durable, and aesthetically pleasing decor that would elevate their property offerings while standing up to high guest turnover. The client required pieces that were stylish enough to appeal to Instagram-savvy travelers, durable enough to withstand frequent use, and versatile enough to work across properties with varying design schemes. Our team curated a custom package of our core decor products, including linen throw pillow covers, seagrass storage baskets, ceramic vases, soy candles, and woven throw blankets, tailored to each property’s specific size and style. We provided bulk pricing and coordinated delivery across all properties over a 6-week period, working directly with their on-site property management team to streamline installation.

Westside Wellness Clinic

Healthcare Facility Ambience Upgrade

Westside Wellness, a holistic healthcare clinic in Portland, Oregon, wanted to transform their clinical space into a warm, calming environment that reduced patient anxiety and felt more welcoming than traditional medical facilities. They needed decor that was non-toxic, easy to clean, and aligned with their brand focus on natural health and sustainability. Our design team collaborated with their clinic director to select a curated selection of products, including our dimmable LED table lamps, macramé wall hangings, marble and acacia wood serving boards for the patient waiting area, unscented soy candles, and small ceramic vases for each exam room. All products were chosen for their non-toxic materials, low-maintenance requirements, and calming, natural aesthetic.

Haven Collective Co-Working Space

Shared Workspace Decor Refresh

Haven Collective, a women-focused co-working space in Brooklyn, New York, wanted to refresh their 12,000 square foot facility to create a more home-like, inspiring environment that separated their offering from traditional corporate co-working spaces. They needed decor pieces that worked for both shared common areas and private office suites, were durable enough for high-traffic use, and aligned with their community-focused, sustainable brand values. Cultyn provided a mix of products including rattan floating shelves for each office, linen throw pillows for lounge areas, woven seagrass storage baskets for shared supplies, ceramic table vases with fresh flower arrangements, and macramé wall hangings for large common area walls. We also worked with their team to create custom branded candle gifts for their annual member appreciation event.
