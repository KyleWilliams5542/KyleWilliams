About Us

FJHEAT LLC is a specialized women's footwear retail destination dedicated to curating stylish, comfortable, and on-trend shoes for every occasion. We prioritize quality craftsmanship, inclusive sizing, and accessible design to help women express their personal style without sacrificing all-day wearability.

Founded in 2019, FJHEAT LLC was born from a simple observation: too many women's footwear options force a choice between looking good and feeling good. Our team of fashion experts and footwear designers travels globally to source and curate a diverse selection of shoes that meet our strict standards for comfort, durability, and style relevance. We offer inclusive sizing from US 5 to 12, wide width options for select styles, and a transparent, customer-first shopping experience that includes free returns and personalized fit consultations. Beyond selling shoes, we aim to build a community of women who feel confident and supported in every step they take, whether they are running errands, heading to the office, or celebrating a special milestone. We are committed to ethical manufacturing practices, partnering only with factories that meet fair labor standards and use sustainable materials whenever possible.

Case Studies

Downtown Chicago Corporate Offices

Employee Comfort Footwear Program

A 200-person corporate firm in downtown Chicago approached FJHEAT LLC in 2022 to create a customized footwear program for their employees, who reported foot pain and discomfort from wearing dress shoes for long office days. Our team worked with the company's HR department to offer exclusive discounts on our Lumina Block Heel Pumps and Classic Leather Loafers, as well as hosted virtual fit workshops to help employees select the right size and style for their needs. We also provided bulk orders of shoe care kits and extended return windows for all program participants.

Coastal Wedding Planners Collective

Wedding Party Footwear Partnership

The Coastal Wedding Planners Collective, a group of 12 high-end wedding planning firms operating in Florida and California, partnered with FJHEAT LLC in 2023 to provide recommended footwear options for brides, grooms, and wedding parties. We created a custom curated collection of our Elegance Stiletto Evening Heels, Breeze Flat Sandals, and Lumina Block Heel Pumps in wedding-friendly colors, with exclusive discounts for clients of the collective and dedicated customer support for bulk wedding party orders. We also offered custom dyeing services for select styles to match wedding color palettes.

National Walking Club Association

Official Footwear Provider for Charity Walks

The National Walking Club Association, which organizes annual charity walking events across 30 U.S. cities with over 15,000 total participants, selected FJHEAT LLC as their official footwear provider in 2024. We supplied our ActiveSupport Walking Shoes to event volunteers and offered exclusive discounts to all participants, with 10% of every purchase donated back to the association's charity partners focused on cardiovascular health research. We also hosted pre-walk fit sessions at each event to help participants select the right size and ensure proper fit for long walks.
