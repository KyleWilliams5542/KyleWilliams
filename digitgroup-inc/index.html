About Us

DIGITGROUP INC is a leading hardware retailer dedicated to delivering high-quality, reliable products for both DIY enthusiasts and professional construction contractors. With over 15 years of industry experience, we maintain an extensive inventory of tools, fasteners, building materials, and home improvement supplies to support projects of every scale, from small household repairs to large commercial construction jobs.

Founded in 2008, DIGITGROUP INC began as a small local hardware store serving residential customers in the Pacific Northwest, and has grown into a regional supplier trusted by over 20,000 professionals and DIYers across 12 states. We pride ourselves on combining a carefully curated product selection with exceptional customer service, including expert product advice, flexible delivery options, and competitive pricing that works for both one-time buyers and ongoing commercial accounts. Our team includes experienced construction and home improvement specialists who personally test and vet every product we carry, ensuring that every item on our shelves meets our strict standards for durability, performance, and safety. We are committed to sustainable business practices, including partnering with manufacturers who use eco-friendly production methods and offering a recycling program for old power tools and metal construction waste. At DIGITGROUP INC, we don’t just sell hardware – we build long-term relationships with our customers by being the reliable resource they can count on for every project, big or small.
