Portable Jobsite Generator - Detailed Description

7,500W portable gasoline generator with electric start, designed to provide power for tools and equipment at remote construction sites or during power outages.

## Product Features

• 420cc OHV engine delivers 9,375W peak power and 11 hours of runtime at 50% load
• Low-oil shutoff and overload protection to prevent engine damage
• Four 120V AC outlets and one 240V outlet to support multiple tools simultaneously
• Noise-reducing muffler limits operation noise to 74 dB for neighborhood-friendly use

## Application Scenarios

Suitable for various industries and usage scenarios, meeting the needs of different customers.
