Knee-High Leather Riding Boots

Timeless knee-high riding boots crafted from full-grain leather, with a classic equestrian-inspired design that pairs perfectly with fall and winter wardrobes for both casual and dressy occasions.

Key Features

  • ✓ Premium full-grain leather upper that develops a natural patina
  • ✓ Adjustable calf buckle to fit 13-17 inch calf circumferences
  • ✓ Full-length side zipper for easy on and off
  • ✓ Padded insole with arch support for all-day comfort
  • ✓ Water-resistant coating for protection against the elements

Product Details

These knee-high riding boots are made from premium full-grain leather that develops a rich, natural patina over time, improving in appearance with regular wear. The full-length side zipper with a top snap closure allows for easy on and off, while the adjustable back calf buckle accommodates calf circumferences from 13 to 17 inches, ensuring a comfortable fit for a wide range of body types. The 1-inch stacked leather heel provides a subtle lift without sacrificing stability, and the padded insole with arch support offers all-day comfort for walking and standing.



The moisture-wicking textile lining prevents sweat buildup and keeps feet dry during temperature changes, while the slip-resistant rubber outsole provides traction on wet and slippery surfaces. The classic, streamlined design features minimal hardware and a rounded toe, making these boots versatile enough to pair with jeans, tights, skirts, and dresses for work, weekends, and special events. Available in black, brown, and tan full-grain leather options, the boots come pre-treated with a water-resistant coating that protects against light rain and snow, reducing the need for frequent reconditioning.
