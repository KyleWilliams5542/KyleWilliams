Case Studies

Employee Footwear Program for Nursing Staff

A 7-hospital regional healthcare network approached BETTRDAY LLC in 2022 to create a custom footwear program for their 1,200+ nursing and clinical staff, who were reporting high rates of foot pain, plantar fasciitis, and lower back issues from spending 12+ hour shifts on their feet. Our team worked with the hospital’s occupational health department to identify appropriate footwear options that met the facility’s non-slip, closed-toe requirements while providing the necessary support for long shifts. We provided bulk discounted pricing on our CloudStep Sneakers and Ballet Comfort Flats, along with virtual sizing workshops and a flexible return policy for staff who needed to exchange sizes after testing the shoes during shifts.

Curated Footwear Collection for Eco-Conscious Retail Pop-Up

The Sustainable Fashion Collective, a non-profit organization that promotes ethical and sustainable clothing brands, partnered with BETTRDAY LLC in 2023 to create a limited-edition footwear collection for their 10-city pop-up tour across the United States. Our team selected 5 of our most sustainably produced styles, including shoes made with recycled plastic uppers, responsibly sourced leather, and carbon-neutral manufacturing processes, and created custom co-branded labeling for the collection. We provided staff training for the pop-up teams on the sustainability features of each style, and donated 10% of all pop-up sales to the Collective’s microgrant program for small BIPOC-owned sustainable fashion brands.

Official Footwear Partner for Annual Conference Event

The organizers of the National Professional Women’s Conference, which hosts 3,000+ attendees annually, selected BETTRDAY LLC as their official footwear partner for their 2024 event, after receiving consistent feedback from past attendees about foot pain from wearing formal shoes during the 3-day conference. We set up a pop-up shop in the conference exhibit hall offering a curated selection of our professional and comfortable footwear styles, including the Lumina Block Heels and Minimalist Leather Mules, along with free foot sizing consultations and on-site purchases that could be shipped directly to attendees’ homes to avoid carrying extra luggage. We also hosted a 30-minute workshop on choosing supportive footwear for professional environments that drew over 400 attendees.

About Us

Founded in 2019, BETTRDAY LLC was born out of a simple frustration: too many women’s footwear brands force customers to choose between looking good and feeling good. Our team of fashion industry experts and podiatry consultants works together to curate a collection that prioritizes both form and function, vetting every design for arch support, durable construction, and contemporary style. We partner with small, ethical manufacturers across Europe, Asia, and North America to ensure fair labor practices and sustainable production processes, while keeping our prices accessible for a wide range of budgets. As a women-founded and led company, we are committed to creating an inclusive shopping experience that caters to all foot sizes, widths, and style preferences, from size 5 to size 12, narrow to wide fits. We believe that when your feet feel good, you feel confident, and we work every day to help our customers step into every occasion with comfort and pride.
