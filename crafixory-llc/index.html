About Us

Crafixory LLC is a trusted comprehensive sports equipment retailer dedicated to delivering premium, performance-driven gear for fitness enthusiasts, team sports athletes, and outdoor adventurers worldwide. We carefully curate our product selection to meet the needs of users at every skill level, from beginners to professional competitors, prioritizing durability, comfort, and innovation in every item we offer. Our customer-centric approach combines competitive pricing with expert product guidance to help every customer achieve their athletic goals and enjoy a safer, more rewarding sports experience.

Founded in 2018, Crafixory LLC was born from a passion for sports and a recognition that athletes of all levels deserve access to reliable, high-quality equipment without overpaying for brand markup. Our team of experienced athletes and product specialists spends hundreds of hours testing every item in our catalog, ensuring each product meets our strict standards for safety, durability, and real-world performance. We work directly with trusted manufacturers to eliminate unnecessary supply chain costs, passing those savings on to our customers while maintaining rigorous quality control. Whether you are setting up your first home gym, gearing up for a competitive league season, or preparing for a backcountry hiking expedition, we are committed to providing personalized support, transparent product information, and a seamless shopping experience that puts your needs first. We also partner with local youth sports programs and community athletic initiatives across the United States, donating 5% of our annual profits to help increase access to sports equipment for underserved communities.

Case Studies

City of Austin Parks & Recreation Department

Community Youth Sports Equipment Upgrade

The City of Austin Parks & Recreation Department needed to fully equip 12 local youth sports leagues serving over 1,800 children aged 6 to 14 across soccer, basketball, baseball, and general fitness programs, with a tight budget and 4-week delivery deadline before the start of the spring season. Crafixory LLC worked closely with the department's program coordinators to select durable, age-appropriate equipment including soccer balls, basketballs, catcher's gear, mouthguards, and first aid kits that met all NFHS safety standards, while staying 15% under their allocated budget. Our team also provided free custom uniform design services for 8 of the leagues, and coordinated a phased delivery schedule to ensure each league received their equipment in time for pre-season practices.

Peak Performance Fitness Center

Home Gym Equipment Bulk Order for Remote Members

When Peak Performance Fitness Center was forced to close their physical location for 6 months during 2020, they wanted to provide their 250 monthly members with access to high-quality home workout equipment to maintain their fitness routines while gyms were inaccessible. Crafixory LLC created a custom package of adjustable dumbbells, yoga mats, resistance bands, and workout guides that was priced at 40% below retail for the gym's members, with individual shipping directly to each member's home. We also provided the gym's trainers with a series of exclusive workout videos tailored to the equipment, which they could share with their members as part of their virtual training program.

Northwest Youth Soccer Academy

Travel Team Uniform and Gear Supply

Northwest Youth Soccer Academy, a competitive travel soccer program with 18 teams and over 300 players, needed a reliable supplier for custom uniforms, training equipment, and player safety gear that could accommodate frequent roster changes and fast turnaround for new players. Crafixory LLC established a dedicated custom portal for the academy, allowing coaches and parents to order uniforms, training balls, cleats, and other gear directly at pre-negotiated discounted rates, with custom logo application and shipping within 7 business days. We also assigned a dedicated account manager to the academy to handle bulk orders for team training equipment and provide size consultations for new age groups each season.
