Product Details

Our Compression Recovery Full Leg Sleeves feature 20-30 mmHg graduated compression that is tightest at the ankle and gradually decreases up the leg, improving blood circulation by up to 40% during physical activity and reducing post-workout muscle soreness and recovery time by up to 30%, according to independent testing. The sleeves are made from a 4-way stretch nylon-spandex blend that is lightweight, moisture-wicking, and breathable, keeping skin dry and comfortable during extended wear, and features flatlock seams that eliminate chafing and irritation even during long runs or multi-hour sports events. The anti-slip silicone band at the top of the sleeve keeps it securely in place without rolling down during movement, eliminating the need for frequent adjustments during activity. They are designed to provide targeted support to the calf, knee, and thigh muscles, reducing risk of strains, shin splints, and knee pain during running, basketball, soccer, weightlifting, and cross training. The sleeves are suitable for both men and women, available in 5 sizes from XS to XXL with a size chart based on calf and thigh circumference to ensure a proper, comfortable fit. They are machine washable and retain their compression level for up to 6 months of regular use, and come with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee if they do not meet your performance expectations. They are also recommended by physical therapists for post-injury recovery and for reducing swelling during long periods of sitting or travel.
