Product Details

Our Non-Slip Premium Yoga Mat is constructed from a dual-layer design with a top layer of textured natural tree rubber that provides superior non-slip grip even when wet with sweat, and a bottom layer of high-density closed-cell foam that prevents moisture absorption and reduces the spread of bacteria. The 6mm thickness provides ideal cushioning for joints during standing, kneeling, and lying poses, while remaining firm enough to maintain stability during balance poses, making it suitable for users of all skill levels from beginners to advanced yoga practitioners. The mat features laser-etched alignment marks that help users properly position their hands and feet, improving form and reducing risk of injury during practice, particularly for those new to yoga or working without an in-person instructor. The mat measures 72 inches long by 24 inches wide, providing extra space for taller users, and weighs just 2.5 pounds, making it easy to roll up and transport to yoga classes or the gym. It is free of PVC, phthalates, and harmful chemical odors, and is 100% biodegradable at the end of its lifespan, making it an eco-friendly choice for environmentally conscious consumers. The mat comes with a complimentary carrying strap and a 1-year warranty against peeling, tearing, and excessive wear, even with daily use. It is suitable for all yoga styles including hot yoga, vinyasa, hatha, and yin, as well as pilates, stretching, and bodyweight home workouts.
