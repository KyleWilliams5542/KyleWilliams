Product Details

Our FDA-Approved High-Impact Sports Mouthguard is made from medical-grade EVA polymer that is BPA, latex, and phthalate-free, ensuring safety for extended use in the mouth. The 6mm thick impact-absorbing core reduces force transmission to the teeth and jaw by 40% during collisions, lowering risk of chipped teeth, jaw fractures, and concussions compared to standard 3mm mouthguards, while the thin, form-fitting outer layer ensures a comfortable fit that does not restrict speech or breathing during play. The mouthguard features a custom moldable design that adapts to your unique dental structure in under 5 minutes using a simple boil-and-bite process, with a re-moldable design that allows you to adjust the fit up to 3 times if needed. It includes a ventilated carrying case with a clip to attach to sports bags, preventing bacteria growth and making it easy to transport between games and practices. The mouthguard is suitable for athletes aged 12 and up, and is approved for use in all major youth and adult contact sports leagues including NCAA, NFHS, and USA Boxing. We offer a $5,000 dental damage guarantee for all users who properly fit and use the mouthguard as directed, providing extra peace of mind for athletes and their families. Custom team color and logo options are available for bulk orders of 20 or more units.
